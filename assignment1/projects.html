Project Overview: Design, Implementation, and Maintenance of a Small Office Network

Objective: To design, implement, and maintain a robust and efficient network for a small branch office.


Network Design:

Architecture: Utilized a three-tier design to ensure scalability and performance.

Redundancy: Deployed two routers connected to different ISPs to provide redundancy and minimize downtime. Implemented IP SLA with tracking to automatically reroute traffic through ISP2 in case ISP1 fails.

Switching: Installed two Cisco Catalyst switches for the LAN, segmenting the network into four VLANs: Human Resources, Executive Office, Sales, and IT.

Security: Integrated ASA firewalls to secure connections to the internet.


End-User Connectivity:

Telephony: Deployed Yealink IP telephones for each end user.

Power over Ethernet (PoE): Utilized PoE ports to connect both PCs and phones to the switches through a single port, simplifying cabling and reducing infrastructure costs.
